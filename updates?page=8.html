Armor and Item Entities Feb 01, 2015

When you're wearing armor, your friends will know about it. I also added item drops so that the things you're mining no longer magically appear in your inventory.

Multiplayer entity management Jan 31, 2015

Playing TrueCraft with your friends is possible, thanks to the new entity management system. It keeps track of which entities should be aware of which other entities and spawns/despawns them as appropriate. With this change also came the very first time any group of TrueCraft enthusiasts played together online. Thanks to that test run, some oversights in the networking code were brought forth and the performance of that code was dramatically improved.
