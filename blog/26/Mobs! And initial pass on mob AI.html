Mobs! And initial pass on mob AI

Published Jul 03, 2015

Mobs can now be spawned on TrueCraft servers, and a simple AI for wandering has been implemented. All beta 1.7.3 mobs are supported - try spawning one with /spawn creeper (or whatever mob you wish to try hanging out with).

This is the initial pass on mobs. Still to come:

  • Performance improvements to pathfinding
  • Verticial pathfinding (moving in the Y direction while wandering)
  • Different AI states (attack, flee) and the wiring that keeps them together
  • AI for special mobs (ghast, slime, squid, etc)

Thanks to Amit for his A* tutorial. Very useful today. I also squeezed some more performance out of the event scheduler, but that's boring so I won't mention it.
