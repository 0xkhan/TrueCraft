Singleplayer support!

Published Jun 03, 2015

Today, TrueCraft has singleplayer support! The launcher has been fleshed out quite a lot and now includes support for keeping a list of your favorite servers, as well as managing your singleplayer worlds and running a server in the background to play them on.

The multiplayer stuff looks like this:

In singleplayer, the launcher is responsible for many of the things that the Minecraft launcher would not do. In Minecraft, the client handles things like browsing singleplayer worlds and managing your server list, but with TrueCraft I thought the launcher was a better place for that.

Coming up soon: better builds of TrueCraft for all platforms, and fleshing out the client's rendering. With the rendering taken care of, I'll be adding chat support to the client and from there we'll have a good place to start implementing other overlays like inventories and so on.
